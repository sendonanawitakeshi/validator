This pipeline watches that relationship in real time, detects when market conditions are shifting, and produces calibrated trading signals for BTC, ETH, SOL, and LINK. Pipeline Capabilities

  • Regime Detection — 60-day rolling window, 4 states (NEUTRAL / CRYPTO_LEADS / SEMI_LEADS / SYSTEMIC). Bottleneck-weighted proximity scoring with velocity adjustment
  • Crypto Calibration — Conditional return distributions P(R|regime,symbol) for BTC/ETH/SOL/LINK. Data-driven confidence, direction optimization, Kelly fractions, cross-symbol correlation matrices
  • Model Tournament — Leave-one-transition-out cross-validation of 5 models (Weibull, Markov, Semi-Markov, Sequential, Naive). Brier decomposition, stacking weights, disagreement index
  • Ensemble Pipeline — Regime change alerts (NONE through CONFIRMED), Bayesian Markov transitions, Weibull survival analysis, sequential evidence accumulation (SPRT+CUSUM+Bayesian CPD), prediction calibration scoring
